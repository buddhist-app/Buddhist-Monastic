Bhikkhu Manual App Version 1.0

May 31st, 2025

New Bhikkhu Manual is an Android app designed to help bhikkhus understand and practice monastic discipline according to the Vinaya.
Main contents:
1. Ordination Procedure – guidelines for sāmaṇera and bhikkhu ordination.
2. Pātimokkha – monastic rules for Pali recitation and Uposatha observance.
3. Pavāraṇā – procedure for the end of Vassa ceremony.
4. Vinaya (Notes) – Provides practical procedures commonly used in the daily life of bhikkhus.
5. Chanting (Paritta) – Chants for daily and ceremonial use.
6. Uposatha Schedule – calendar of monastic observances.
